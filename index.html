About

Mick Jeets is a Denver-based electronic music artist melding nostalgia and emotion to form his cutting-edge sound.

Paying homage to house, dubstep, and breakbeat, Mick Jeets's multigenre sets are as dynamic as they are immersive — equally suited for an intimate club setting or a festival stage. With each production, Mick Jeets crafts vivid sonic worldscapes that summon deep emotional resonance while delivering undeniable dancefloor energy, drawing from nature’s boundless beauty. Inspired by boundary-pushing artists like Four Tet, Skrillex, and Caribou, Mick Jeets's performances bring people together, creating unforgettable moments that transcend the music itself.

A rising presence in Denver's electronic music scene, Mick Jeets has already left a mark: he's played at iconic venues like Meow Wolf and The Black Box, as well as supported acts such as Nikki Nair, Mary Droppinz, Todd Edwards, and Conducta. From dreamy soundscapes to chunky bass bangers, Mick Jeets bridges nostalgia and innovation, channeling the introspective depths and euphoric highs that define electronic music.
